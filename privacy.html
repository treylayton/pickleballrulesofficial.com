Privacy Policy

Effective March 25, 2026

The Pickleball Official does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything runs entirely on your device.

Overview

The Pickleball Official ("the App") is a pickleball rules reference app developed by Centrifical Apps. We built this app with a simple privacy philosophy: your data is yours. We don't want it, and we don't collect it.

Data Collection

The App collects no data from its users. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No device identifiers
  • No cookies or similar technologies
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

On-Device Processing

All features of the App, including the decision trees and any Apple Intelligence integrations, run entirely on your device. No data is sent to our servers because we don't have servers that receive user data.

Apple Intelligence

The App may use Apple Intelligence features to help you find the right ruling using natural language. This processing is handled entirely by Apple's on-device intelligence framework. We do not receive, store, or have access to any queries or results from this processing.

Subscriptions

The App offers a subscription that is managed entirely through Apple's App Store. Payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information, Apple ID, or any purchase details beyond what Apple provides to developers in aggregate, anonymized sales reports.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data from any users, we also collect no data from children. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new effective date. Given our commitment to collecting no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
